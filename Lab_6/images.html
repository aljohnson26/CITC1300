Go on a hiking adventure!

The 28-mile multi-use trail system encircles a 528-acre reservoir supporting TVA's Raccoon Mountain Pumped Storage generating plant

Located on public lands managed by TVA, the trails have become a popular destination for hikers and mountain bikers from around the region.
